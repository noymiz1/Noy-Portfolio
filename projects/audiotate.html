Audiotate

2021

Audiotate is a music annotation digital platform that allows users from all music streaming apps to capture and revisit music as memories. It was created in Harvard University's Design for Desirability course. It includes features like: personal lyric annotations, song-based journaling, customizable tags, cross-platform playlist organization

Audiotate provides a platform for intimate music discovery, archiving, and sharing.

Created in collaboration with Brenda Ceja, Daniela Villafuerte, Diana Zhu, Halima Badri, Kara Xie, and Sara-Ann Yong. My contribution came in the form of app and identity design for the application.
