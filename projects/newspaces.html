New Spaces of Justice: DV

2021

Developed in a multidisciplinary workshop in collaboration with Oana Stanescu, Virgil Abloh, Nóra Al Haider, and the Legal Design Lab at Stanford University. We assembled a team of designers and lawyers to reimagine court proceedings in the Zoom era, focusing specifically on domestic violence hearings. Through comprehensive research, surveys with court administrators like Clemens A. Landau, the presiding judge of Utah's Salt Lake City Justice Court, and engagement with advocacy groups and victims of domestic violence, we proposed adding a questionnaire for all case participants to address and mitigate privacy and security concerns for the victims.

This project was a collaborative effort with Shaina Yang, Calvin Boyd, Gregory Ng Yong H, and Paola Méndez. All graphics and slides were created by me.
