Superbloom

2023

I teamed up with Boston-artist Deborah Johnson to craft a unique table for her installation, Superbloom, a project supported by the City of Boston. Using Fusion360, I modeled out the design, and computer-aided machining on the Shopbot, cut out the wooden pieces for assembly. Special attention was given to integrating custom components to bolster structural integrity.

Designed for adaptability, the table can be conveniently disassembled and reassembled, making it ideal for Superbloom's sessions across various Boston parks. A unique feature of the table is its "lazy susan"-style tabletop, promoting communal engagement in art activities. Post-fabrication, I guided Deborah on the best finishing techniques for the wood, and she beautifully accentuated the table with her signature red designs.
