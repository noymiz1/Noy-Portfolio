Bounty Carsheets

2022

Added a new product to the Bounty paper towel line in the form of Carsheets. The product was developed in the Harvard GSD seminar, Paper or Plastic: Reinventing Shelf Life in the Supermarket Landscape. In which we were asked to operate as brand strategists. However, rather than invent new products, students will instead innovate upon existing brands.

We worked as a group to reconsider the entire Bounty brand including: brand architecture, brand identity, consumer target, logo, tagline, packaging, etc. We also studied our product's shelf competitors and presented our observations through visual arguments. My group and I did extensive market research, user profiling, and design iteration to reach the final product presented.

Created in collaboration with Ruiheng Li, Sara-Ann Yong, Oliver Shi, Zhihan Wang, and Yabby Abejie. My contribution is in the form of brand identity and packaging design seen below.
