A Knot

2022

A Knot is a short film I developed during Harvard University's Experimental Animation course. Drawing inspiration from a 3rd-century Coptic textile in the Harvard Art Museums collection, this project explores the concept of the Ouroboros, aiming to create an endless loop of motion using a variety of materials.

The film was crafted through stop-motion animation over the course of many hours, using a light box, sand, fake worms, yarn, and shoes.
