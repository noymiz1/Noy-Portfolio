Enduring Heirlooms

A platform for tracking and mapping family heirlooms to rediscover cultural identity

UX/UI Design Cultural Research Storytelling 2021-2023

Project Overview

Enduring Heirlooms is a digital platform concept that helps users map lost cultural identity by building a provenance for their family heirlooms. The platform encourages open access ownership of heirlooms and connects users through shared origin stories.

This project began as a personal journey to track the origins of my family's amulet. By meeting with heirloom collectors and researching similar artifacts, I was able to trace its origins to a specific time and place. This inspired the idea for a digital platform that could help others uncover the stories behind their own heirlooms.

The Problem

Many individuals with fragmented cultural identities struggle to map their lineage due to limited access to historical records or lost cultural artifacts.

Disconnected Heirlooms

Family heirlooms often lack context, leaving their origins and significance unknown.

Fragmented Histories

Users struggle to piece together their family histories due to incomplete records.

Limited Collaboration

Existing tools don't allow users to collaborate or share data to uncover shared histories.

Impact

Enduring Heirlooms empowers users to reconnect with their cultural identity and collaborate with others to uncover shared histories.

By combining storytelling with data visualization, the platform bridges the gap between personal history and collective heritage. It has the potential to create a global archive of cultural artifacts, fostering collaboration and understanding across communities.
